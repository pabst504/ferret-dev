Make the Bus is Fancy Ferret Games' debut title. Built in Godot 4, it drops you into a top-down neighborhood where your only goal is to reach the bus stop before the bell rings.

Along the way you'll need to avoid distracted drivers, territorial dogs, and the ever-present threat of the school bully. Every run is a new route through the chaos.
